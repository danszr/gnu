I am a GNU. Here is me when I was a wild beast wandering around aimlessly with nothing to do. Those were tough times. Thankfully, the Free Software Foundation headed by Richard Stallman brought me out of solitude so I could live the good life.

Back in the day when I was a baby, I hung out with Baby Tux, the mascot of the Linux Kernel. Those were fun times when we first got together. However, our parents, Richard Stallman and Linus Torvalds, didn't quite get along...

Here, I'm listening to music from a CD player. I feel really bad for all of those people who listen to music from Apple's DRM-infested music players. Those people deserve better. :(

Me and Tux are all grown up and are supporting the GNU Public License together. We will fight to the death anyone who threatens the GPL or the Free Software Movement. It feels good to be flying.
